# LC-3.3 Customer Care & Support Services

## Purpose
Provide comprehensive technical support, documentation, training, and operational assistance to airlines and operators throughout the aircraft operational life.

## Scope

This phase delivers world-class customer support, including:

- **IETP / S1000D Modules:** Interactive Electronic Technical Publications
- **Technical Documentation:** Comprehensive maintenance and operations manuals
- **Spares & Provisioning:** Parts supply and inventory management
- **IPC Updates:** Illustrated Parts Catalog maintenance
- **Operational Assistance:** 24/7 technical support and troubleshooting
- **Training:** Maintenance, operations, and specialized systems training
- **Cabin Systems Support:** Passenger experience and cabin systems
- **APAS Advisory:** Auxiliary Power and Propulsion Systems guidance

## Key Activities

### 1. Technical Documentation

#### Core Documentation Suite

##### Aircraft Flight Manual (AFM/RFM)
- Operating limitations
- Normal procedures
- Emergency procedures
- Performance data
- Weight and balance
- Systems descriptions

##### Aircraft Maintenance Manual (AMM)
- System descriptions
- Maintenance practices
- Troubleshooting procedures
- Servicing and lubrication
- Special tools and equipment
- Adjustment and test procedures

##### Illustrated Parts Catalog (IPC)
- Exploded view diagrams
- Part numbers and nomenclature
- Applicability and effectivity
- Ordering information
- Interchange and substitution data

##### Structural Repair Manual (SRM)
- Repair procedures for airframe damage
- Allowable damage limits
- Temporary and permanent repairs
- Composite repair techniques
- Corrosion treatment

##### Wiring Diagram Manual (WDM)
- Electrical system schematics
- Circuit diagrams
- Connector pinouts
- Wire routing and installation
- Troubleshooting aids

##### Component Maintenance Manual (CMM)
- Component-specific maintenance
- Overhaul procedures
- Testing and calibration
- Parts breakdown
- Repair limits

#### S1000D Implementation
- **Modular Documentation:**
  - Data modules for reusability
  - Common Source Database (CSDB)
  - Publication module assembly
  - Multiple output formats (PDF, HTML, IETP)

- **Metadata & Classification:**
  - Standard Numbering System (SNS)
  - Data Module Code (DMC)
  - Information Codes (IC)
  - Applicability and effectivity metadata

- **Common Information Repository (CIR):**
  - Multimedia objects (graphics, photos, videos)
  - Warnings, cautions, and notes
  - Standard terminology and definitions
  - Reusable procedural data modules

#### Interactive Electronic Technical Publications (IETP)
- **Features:**
  - Hyperlinked navigation
  - Interactive 3D models
  - Search and filtering
  - Task-oriented content
  - Personalization by operator/aircraft configuration

- **Offline/Online Capability:**
  - Downloadable for offline use
  - Cloud-based for real-time updates
  - Mobile device compatibility (tablets, smartphones)
  - AR (Augmented Reality) integration for maintenance tasks

### 2. Technical Support

#### 24/7 Customer Support Center (CSC)
- **Contact Channels:**
  - Phone hotline
  - Email support
  - Web portal / ticketing system
  - Live chat
  - Video conferencing for complex issues

- **Support Tiers:**
  - **Tier 1:** Basic troubleshooting, documentation access
  - **Tier 2:** Advanced technical support, engineering escalation
  - **Tier 3:** OEM engineering, design authority involvement

- **Response Times:**
  - Critical (AOG): Immediate response, 24/7
  - High Priority: Response within 2-4 hours
  - Standard: Response within 24 hours

## Best Practices

- **Customer-Centric:** Tailor support to individual operator needs
- **Proactive Communication:** Anticipate issues and inform customers early
- **24/7 Availability:** Always accessible for critical support
- **Continuous Improvement:** Learn from every interaction and feedback
- **Digital First:** Leverage modern tools (IETP, AR, mobile apps)
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Build a comprehensive knowledge base
- **Training Excellence:** Invest in high-quality training programs and instructors
- **Partnership Approach:** Work collaboratively with operators as partners, not just customers
