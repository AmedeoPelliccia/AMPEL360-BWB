## Overview

The AMPEL360 BWB is a next-generation hydrogen-electric blended wing body aircraft that combines:
- **Hydrogen Propulsion:** Zero-emission cryogenic hydrogen fuel systems
- **Electric Distributed Propulsion:** Multiple electric motors for efficiency and redundancy
- **AI/NN Orchestration:** Neural network systems for flight control, environmental control, and avionics management
- **Circular Economy:** End-to-end material traceability and lifecycle sustainability
- **Digital Thread:** Complete traceability from design through operations

## Key Features

### Novel Technologies
- **Hydrogen Systems (ATA 28H):** Cryogenic storage and distribution
- **Electric Propulsion (ATA 80):** Distributed electric motors
- **Green-E Battery (ATA 85):** Advanced energy storage with lifecycle management
- **AI/NN Systems (ATA 95):** NN-ECS, NN-FCS, NN-IMA orchestration
- **BWB Configuration:** Blended wing body aerodynamics and structures

### Digital Engineering
- **OPT-IN Framework:** Organization, Program, Technical, Infrastructure, Neural Networks
- **UTCS:** Universal Traceability & Configuration System
- **GenCCC:** Generative Configuration & Compliance Checking
- **CGen:** Automated Content Generation
- **Digital Twin:** Real-time model synchronization

### Certification Alignment
- **ARP4754A:** Aircraft and System Development
- **ARP4761:** Safety Assessment Process
- **DO-178C/DO-254:** Software and Hardware Certification
- **DO-160:** Environmental Qualification
- **CS-25/FAR Part 25:** Transport Category Airworthiness
