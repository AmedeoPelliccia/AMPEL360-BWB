## 1. Purpose

Baseline C represents an aggressive technology scenario based on 2031 maturity (TRL 8-9), maximizing performance with higher programmatic risk.

---

## 2. Key Improvements Over Baselines A and B

| Parameter | Baseline A | Baseline B | Baseline C |
|-----------|------------|------------|------------|
| **MTOW** | 65,000 kg | 62,000 kg | 59,000 kg |
| **FC power density** | 1.2 kW/kg | 1.5 kW/kg | 1.8 kW/kg |
| **Battery density** | 400 Wh/kg | 400 Wh/kg | 450 Wh/kg |
| **H₂ storage** | GH₂ 350 | GH₂ 700/LH₂ | LH₂ |
| **Range** | 1,500 nm | 1,650 nm | 1,800 nm |

## 3. Technology Drivers

- **Fuel cells:** 1.8 kW/kg (aerospace-optimized stacks)
- **H₂ storage:** Liquid hydrogen (LH₂) baseline, cryogenic systems mature
- **Battery:** 450 Wh/kg (solid-state or advanced Li-ion)
- **AI control:** Full authority with extensive certification

## 4. Risk Assessment

**Overall Risk:** High  
**Technology Dependency:** Multiple immature technologies must converge  
**EIS Target:** 2035

**Recommendation:** Monitor as technology roadmap option, not primary baseline.
