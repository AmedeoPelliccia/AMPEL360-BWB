# LC-11_0000-GENERAL_Q100-CONCEPT-BASELINES

## Overview

This directory contains the baseline concept definitions and supporting documentation for the AMPEL360 BWB Q100 aircraft. These documents establish the foundational design parameters, assumptions, and decisions for the Q100 program.

## Document Structure

### Concept Foundation (01-07)
- **01** — Concept Overview: High-level description of the Q100 aircraft
- **02** — Architecture Rationale: Technical justification for key design decisions
- **03** — Concept Evolution Log: Chronological history of concept development
- **04** — Geometry Definition: External dimensions and geometric parameters
- **05** — Planform Layout: Top-view wing and control surface arrangement
- **06** — Cabin Layout Concept: Interior configuration and passenger accommodation
- **07** — Crew and Passenger Flow: Boarding, operations, and egress concepts

### Mission and Performance (08-11)
- **08** — Mission and Payload Assumptions: Design mission profiles and payload requirements
- **09** — PMB0 Preliminary Mass Breakdown: Weight budget and mass properties
- **10** — Performance Assumptions: Aerodynamic and propulsive performance parameters
- **11** — Energy Sizing Preliminary: Hydrogen fuel and battery system sizing

### Baseline Configurations (12-16)
- **12** — Baseline A Definition: Conservative technology baseline (2027 TRL)
- **13** — Baseline B Definition: Moderate technology advancement (2029 TRL)
- **14** — Baseline C Definition: Aggressive technology scenario (2031 TRL)
- **15** — Baseline Comparison: Comparative analysis of three baselines
- **16** — Baseline Selection Rationale: Justification for baseline choice

### Trade Studies and Parameters (17-19)
- **17** — Trade Study Input Assumptions: Parameters for trade analyses
- **18** — Parameter Bounds: Design space constraints and limits
- **19** — Key Open Questions: Unresolved technical and programmatic questions

### Systems Integration (20-23)
- **20** — Airframe Integration: Structural integration concepts
- **21** — Propulsion Integration: Distributed propulsion installation
- **22** — ECS and Cabin Integration: Environmental control system
- **23** — GSE and Cargo Concept: Ground support equipment and cargo handling

### Risk Assessment (24-26)
- **24** — Technology Risks: Technology maturation and development risks
- **25** — Structural and Energy Risks: Airframe and energy system specific risks
- **26** — Operations and Certification Risks: Operational and regulatory risks

### Tracking and Documentation (27-28)
- **27** — Concept Decision Log: Major design decisions and rationale
- **28** — Assumptions Register: Catalog of all design assumptions

## Usage

These documents are intended for:
- **Architecture Board:** Configuration management and decision-making
- **Engineering Teams:** Design reference and requirements derivation
- **Program Management:** Schedule, cost, and risk planning
- **Certification Authorities:** Regulatory engagement and compliance planning
