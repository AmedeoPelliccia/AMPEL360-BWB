# LC-11_ATA-0000-GENERAL_Q100-ARCHIVES

## Overview

This directory contains archived snapshots of Q100 concept artefacts, including frozen baselines, superseded studies, obsolete requirements, and historical decision records. The archives provide full traceability for configuration management, decision provenance, and compliance evidence.

## Purpose

The archives serve multiple critical functions:
1. **Historical Traceability:** Preserve decision context and evolution
2. **Configuration Management:** Document what changed, when, and why
3. **Compliance Evidence:** Support certification and audit requirements
4. **Knowledge Retention:** Capture lessons learned and rationale
5. **Governance:** Enable Architecture Board and CAB oversight

## Archival Criteria

Artefacts are archived when:
1. **Baseline Freeze:** Formal freeze of a concept baseline (B0, B1, B2)
2. **Superseded:** Replaced by newer version or alternative approach
3. **Obsolete:** No longer relevant due to concept direction change
4. **Completed:** Studies, reviews, or activities fully completed
5. **Transitioned:** Requirements or risks moved to formal systems (LC-13+)
