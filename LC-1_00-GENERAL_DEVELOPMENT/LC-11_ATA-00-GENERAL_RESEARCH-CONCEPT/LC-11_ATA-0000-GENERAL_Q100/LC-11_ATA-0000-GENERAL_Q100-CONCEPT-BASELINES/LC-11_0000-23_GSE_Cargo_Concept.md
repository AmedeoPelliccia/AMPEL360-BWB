## 1. Purpose

Defines ground support equipment (GSE) requirements and cargo handling concepts for Q100 operations.

---

## 2. Ground Support Equipment

### 2.1 Hydrogen Refueling

- **Equipment:** Mobile H₂ refueling truck or hydrant system
- **Pressure:** 350 bar (from 450 bar ground supply)
- **Flow rate:** 100-150 kg/min
- **Refueling time:** 15-20 minutes (2,500 kg capacity)
- **Safety:** 50 m exclusion zone, automated leak detection

### 2.2 Electrical Ground Power

- **GPU:** 400 kW ground power unit (DC or AC)
- **Battery charging:** 2,000 kW fast charger (30-minute recharge)
- **Connection:** High-voltage DC connector (800 VDC)

### 2.3 Standard GSE

- **Tow tractor:** Pushback and towing
- **Belt loaders:** Cargo loading (2× for forward/aft doors)
- **Air conditioning:** Ground cooling unit (hot weather)
- **Lavatory service:** Waste removal truck
- **Potable water:** Water replenishment

---

## 3. Cargo System

### 3.1 Cargo Hold Configuration

- **Location:** Underbelly, forward and aft of main landing gear
- **Volume:** ~25 m³ total
- **Access:** Two cargo doors (port side, forward and aft)

### 3.2 Container Compatibility

- **Type:** LD3-45 equivalent (smaller profile for BWB)
- **Capacity:** ~3 containers
- **Weight:** 2,000 kg checked baggage + 500 kg additional cargo

### 3.3 Loading System

- **Manual loading:** Belt loader to cargo door
- **Restraint:** Cargo nets and tie-down straps
- **Loading time:** 10-15 minutes

---

## 4. Turnaround Operations

### 4.1 Standard Turnaround (30-45 min)

1. **Arrival + parking:** 2 min
2. **Passenger deplaning:** 10 min
3. **Cabin cleaning:** 10 min
4. **H₂ refueling:** 15-20 min (parallel)
5. **Cargo unload/load:** 10-15 min (parallel)
6. **Battery recharge:** 30 min (parallel, optional)
7. **Passenger boarding:** 15-20 min
8. **Pushback:** 2 min

### 4.2 Quick Turnaround (20-25 min)

- Light cleaning only
- Partial refuel if not full mission
- No battery recharge (use on-board capacity)
- Optimized boarding procedures

---

## 5. Airport Compatibility

### 5.1 Gate Requirements

- **ICAO Code:** C (wingspan < 52 m)
- **Apron space:** Standard for Code C aircraft
- **Jetway:** Single or dual jetway capable

### 5.2 Infrastructure

- **Hydrogen:** Specialized H₂ refueling (not all airports initially)
- **Electrical:** High-power charging capability (800 VDC, 2 MW)
- **Standard services:** All conventional GSE compatible
