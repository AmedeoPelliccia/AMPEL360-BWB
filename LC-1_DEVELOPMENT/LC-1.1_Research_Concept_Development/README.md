# LC-1.1 Research & Concept Development

## Purpose
Early-stage ideation and conceptual design activities that establish the foundation for the AMPEL360 BWB aircraft program.

## Scope

This phase encompasses the initial exploration and conceptualization of the hydrogen-electric BWB aircraft, including:

- **Ideation & Prompt Engineering:** AI-assisted concept generation and exploration
- **Architecture Trades:** Evaluation of alternative architectural approaches
- **Mission Envelopes:** Definition of operational requirements and flight profiles
- **Sizing Loops:** Preliminary aircraft sizing and weight estimation
- **Environmental Impact:** Sustainability targets and carbon footprint analysis
- **Conceptual Risk Assessments:** V0 safety assumptions and early hazard identification

## Key Activities

### 1. Conceptual Design
- BWB configuration studies
- Hydrogen propulsion system concepts
- AI-orchestrated systems architecture
- Cabin layout and passenger experience concepts

### 2. Mission Analysis
- Range and payload requirements
- Flight profile optimization
- Operational scenarios and use cases
- Market analysis and customer requirements

### 3. Technology Assessment
- Hydrogen storage and distribution technology
- Electric propulsion maturity
- AI/NN integration readiness
- Composite materials selection

### 4. Sustainability Analysis
- Life cycle carbon footprint
- Circular economy integration points
- Green-E battery concepts
- CO₂ harvesting potential

### 5. Preliminary Safety
- Initial hazard identification
- Critical failure modes
- Safety-driven design constraints
- V0 safety case outline

## Deliverables

- Concept definition documents
- Trade study reports
- Mission profile specifications
- Preliminary sizing data
- Sustainability assessment reports
- Initial risk register
- Technology readiness assessments

## Tools & Methods

- **Prompt Engineering:** AI-assisted conceptual generation
- **MBSE:** Model-Based Systems Engineering for architecture
- **Sizing Tools:** ADAS, OpenVSP, or custom sizing loops
- **Environmental Analysis:** LCA (Life Cycle Assessment) tools

## Integration

- **Next Phase:** LC-1.2 Safety Analysis builds upon conceptual risk assessments
- **OPT-IN:** Concept artifacts feed into O (Organization) and P (Program) axes
- **UTCS:** Initial identity assignment for conceptual components
- **GenCCC:** Early requirement capture and traceability setup
